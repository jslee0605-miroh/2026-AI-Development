# Draft quality rubric (Lecture 2)

Score each draft 0â€“2 on each dimension. Total out of 10.

## 1) Personalization
- 0: Generic; doesn't reference lead context
- 1: References role/company/industry but shallow
- 2: References specific pain points from notes in a believable way

## 2) Factual correctness / grounding
- 0: Claims facts not in the provided docs
- 1: Mostly grounded; minor questionable statement
- 2: Entirely grounded in lead notes + one-pager; no invented facts

## 3) Compliance
- 0: Violates a compliance tag (e.g., medical advice, guarantees)
- 1: Borderline language but fixable
- 2: Clean

## 4) Brand voice
- 0: Hype/salesy/robotic
- 1: Okay but inconsistent
- 2: Clear, friendly, concise

## 5) Format constraints
- 0: Major violations (too long, missing subject, etc.)
- 1: Minor violations
- 2: Meets constraints
